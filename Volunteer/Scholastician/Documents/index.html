How We Work

Students and volunteering tutors both register independently for our tutoring program. Once we match a student with a tutor, we will send one email to both the student and tutor containing both email addresses, so that they can communicate independetly. We reccomend using Zoom as a video calling platform to communicate buth both the student and tutors can choose their preferred platform together. Afterward, the tutors request volunteer hours from the students, which should be arranged in the call themselves. If you have any issues with your tutoring session, please reach us via email or the contact page on this website.
