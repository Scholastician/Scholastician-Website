About Us

The Scholastician platform promotes communities of students alike to share and learn from each other. As a tutor, you get an opportunity to share a hand in the community and have the ability to get volunteer hours.

  • •Quick and simple, register in under a minute
  • •Convenient platform to teach
  • •Only an internet connection required
  • •Volunteer hours granted
How To Register
  1. 1. Go to the “Tutors” tab on our website
  2. 2. Press the “Register” button
  3. 3. Fill out the following “Tutor Registration” form by providing your name, email address, phone number, and grade level, and applicable test scores
  4. 4. Lastly, submit by pressing the “Register button” at the bottom
How to Login
  • 1. Go to the “Tutors” tab on our website
  • 2. Press the “Login” button
  • 3. Enter your username and password information
  • You will receive an email with you and your tutors’ email addresses. This way you can communicate independently for your tutoring session. Please let us know if you had any issues with your session or need to report the tutor for any issues.

    If you would like to request volunteer hours, screenshot the duration of the Zoom call and email the details to us.

    Please note: Tutoring sessions will have a maximum length of 2 hours. Please do not request more than 2 hours at one time because your request be denied.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I want to cancel my request for a session?

Please email your issue to us and we will get back to you!

What if I have to leave in the middle of my session?

Don't worry! You are not obliged to remain in the session if you have to leave.

Not satisfied with your session?

Let us know. Report the tutor on our website and send the details of your concern. We greatly appreciate your feedback!

What if I cannot find a match?

If you do not get matched within one day of submitting your request, please submit another request. If you have any questions, please email us.

Matched with someone you don’t want?

Resubmit our form. You may have other options for tutors.

Do I need to use Zoom?

You can communicate with your tutor through many other video/ calling applications... even through texting! We only recommend Zoom for a ubiquitous means of communication, but everything is really up to you!
